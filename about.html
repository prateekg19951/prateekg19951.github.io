About Pixel Arcade

Hey! I'm Prateek Gupta — a 22-year-old indie developer from India who still believes the best games fit in 50 KB.

In February 2025 I gave myself a crazy challenge: build a full retro gaming portal in just 48 hours. Zero budget. Zero team. Just coffee, nostalgia, and way too many tabs open.

The result? This little corner of the internet you're on right now.

Why I built this

I missed the days when:

  • You could play a game instantly — no install, no account, no nonsense
  • Pixel art was king and chiptune music made everything 10× cooler
  • High scores actually meant something

So I collected 50+ of the best free HTML5 pixel games, wrapped them in a cozy beige blanket, and called it Pixel Arcade.

What you'll find here

  • Hand-picked retro games (platformers, puzzles, shooters, RPGs — you name it)
  • Useful tools for pixel artists and game devs
  • Honest game reviews and dev stories on the blog
  • Fresh pixel memes every week (because life is too short for boring feeds)

100% free forever

Every single game is free. No paywalls. No “energy”. No dark patterns.
The site stays alive thanks to tiny, non-annoying Google AdSense ads.
